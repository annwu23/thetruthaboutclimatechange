How to Help

  1. Be active:

    Donate to environmental groups! Speak out on Facebook! There are so many different ways you can actively help stop climate change. Every single person counts in spreading the message!
    Psst! This site lists 19 charities, 7 for the environment specifically and 12 for other admirable causes, from animal welfare to coral reefs.



  2. Be efficient:

    Most people switch off the lights when they leave a room… and do nothing else. How can you be efficient beyond that? When you don't use electronics, unplug them. Use cold, not hot, water to wash your clothes. And, of course, look for the most energy-saving lights possible — fluorescents, LEDs…



  3. Choose environment-friendly commute:

    How do you get around the city? Most people would answer, car. But car exhaust accounts for a huge percentage of human-caused greenhouse gases, so why not try something else? If you have a bike, bike — it's great exercise that also helps the planet. If you don't, why not take the metro? There are much greener ways to get around than driving!



  4. Don't waste:

    Did you know that each year, the total amount of trash in the world amounts to 1.3 billion tons? The garbage you produce is a huge creator of methane (one of the potent greenhouse gases I mention on this site). What can you do about it? Remember the three R's you learned in school: Reuse, Reduce, Recycle. You can also form a compost pile with food that you'd otherwise trash, such as banana peels; the compost can be used as fertilizer for plants. Plus, why not buy food that has minimal or biodegradable packaging?
