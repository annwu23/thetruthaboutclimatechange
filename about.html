This site, launched in 2017, has a single goal: to be a website where climate change information isn't sugarcoated or glossed over, and where both environment lovers and non-tree-huggers can go for solid fact. If all you want is to know what's going on with carbon dioxide and what you can do, then this site is for you!


Who Am I?

Anne Wu is currently a 12-year-old seventh grade student at Kaohsiung American School, in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. She has loved animals (particularly cats) almost all her life, and has been interested in the environment and climate change ever since fourth or fifth grade. When alone, she likes to read and write stories. She also plays the piano and violin.
